LED projects that define the space they live in

Some installations call for more than a standard package: a curved wall in a hotel lobby, an immersive environment in a brand experience center, an architectural feature suspended above a retail flagship. 212Visual co-delivers these projects with the firms leading them, providing the engineering depth, hardware sourcing, and on-site execution that complex installations require.

Talk to our team →

Every environment, every form factor.

Curved and architectural LED
Curved, wrapped, and freeform installations that follow the architecture of the space rather than imposing on it.
Custom shapes
Ribbons, spheres, domes, cubes, columns, and canopies, engineered for unique installations and bespoke environments.
Immersive environments
Multi-surface, multi-angle installations for brand experience centers, museums, and flagship retail.
Transparent and OLED features
See-through LED and OLED for windows, partitions, and layered visual experiences that preserve light and sightlines.
LED tunnels and ceilings
Overhead and tunnel installations that transform how a space feels from the point of entry.
Interactive and touch-enabled
Touch-responsive walls and interactive display systems for retail, education, and brand experience applications.

Turnkey: from concept to commissioning in one engagement

Define the vision
212Visual works with the lead firm and the end client to understand the space, the experience goals, and the constraints before specifications are written.
Engineering and specifications
Environmental specs, CAD and DWG documentation, and content concepts that support the proposal and inform the install.
Hardware and sourcing
High-performance LED hardware, custom mounting systems, and specialty components sourced through established manufacturing partnerships.
Installation and sign-off
Installation oversight, system calibration, and acceptance testing, with the client seeing the system performing before the team leaves the site.

Hardware specified for performance over the life of the installation.

Engineered for the environment
Hardware specified by pixel pitch, brightness, and viewing distance for the space it will live in.
Tested before it ships
Burn-in testing, color calibration, and quality control on every system before it leaves the manufacturer.
Supported for the long term
Spare parts, replacement panels, and service infrastructure in place from day one.
Sourced through direct partnerships
Strategic relationships with established LED manufacturers, structured around quality rather than price.
