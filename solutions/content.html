Content engineered for the LED installation it runs on.

212Visual operates an in-house creative team that produces 2D and 3D motion content, branded experiences, data displays, and ongoing content programs for LED installations. Every engagement begins with the technical specs of the wall and the goals for the space, so the content performs the way the hardware was designed to.

Content built for the wall, the environment, and the goals it serves.

Every content engagement starts with the specs of the installation: hardware configuration, pixel pitch, brightness, viewing conditions, and the experience the space is meant to deliver. From there, the creative work follows.

2D and 3D motion graphics

Resolution-native content engineered to the exact pixel count of the installation.

Branded lobby and experience content

Visual content for public-facing displays that represents the brand at the scale of the space.

Campaign-based content programs

Content that refreshes alongside the client's marketing calendar, keeping the installation current.

Data-driven and real-time displays

KPI dashboards, live feeds, operational displays, and real-time integrations for corporate and command center environments.

Template systems and content libraries

Self-service update systems that let internal teams refresh content without external production support.

Available as a standalone service

212Visual provides content for any LED installation, regardless of who supplied the hardware.

The wall is the investment. The content is the return.

An LED installation performs commercially when the content is engineered to the same standard as the hardware. 212Visual designs content alongside the technical specifications of the wall, so resolution, brightness, viewing distance, and brand goals all inform the creative work from the start. The result is a visual system that delivers on the investment from day one.
