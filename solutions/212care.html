212Care

The wall should never be
the reason for the call.

Your client bought an LED wall to impress visitors, communicate with employees, or transform a space. They didn't buy it to generate service tickets. Without proactive monitoring and a clear support plan, that's exactly what happens — and the call comes to you.

Learn about 212Care plans Talk to our service team →
What 212Care includes

Proactive. Not reactive.

Every service call you don't have to make is time back in your business. 212Care reduces your post-install support burden by catching problems early — before your client notices them.

Remote system health monitoring
Continuous telemetry monitoring with automated alerts — so we know about a panel issue before your client does.
Proactive diagnostics
We flag issues before they cause downtime. Most problems are resolved remotely, before they become a site visit.
SLA-based service tiers
Response windows matched to your client's uptime requirements — from standard installations to high-traffic, high-visibility environments.
Transparent warranty management
Batch-matched spare parts, clear warranty terms, and a defined claims process — no surprises, no chasing down a manufacturer overseas.
Defined escalation paths
Every support scenario has a clear owner and a defined next step. Nothing falls through the cracks at 11pm before a client event.
Quarterly performance reviews
Regular check-ins on system health, content performance, and upcoming maintenance needs — available on Enhanced and Premium tiers.
