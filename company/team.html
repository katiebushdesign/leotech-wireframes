We look for professionals who bring depth to the work.

212Visual is structured around the standard set out in the 212° philosophy: preparation, precision, and care across every engagement. The people who do their best work here share an interest in technical depth, a respect for the client relationship, and the discipline to deliver complex installations to a documented standard.

Technical depth
Engineering, content, and service roles that require expertise across LED hardware, signal management, content production, or commercial system operations.
Client-facing experience
Account management, sales engineering, and partner-facing roles that work alongside the firms delivering 212Visual installations to their end clients.
Operational discipline
Project management, documentation, and operations roles that maintain the standards and infrastructure behind every 212Visual engagement.
